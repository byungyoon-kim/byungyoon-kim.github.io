Territory Planning Data Pipeline

Problem Statement (BizOps)

Territory planning requires accurate financial data for 1,400+ accounts. The previous process was entirely manual, requiring staff to search, verify, and copy-paste data for each company. This 671-hour manual workload (2 people for 2 weeks) created a massive bottleneck for strategic execution.

Product Solution & Execution

  • Process Redesign: Identified that data collection was the primary bottleneck in territory planning. Designed an automated workflow to replace manual entry.
  • Technical Execution: Engineered a Python/Selenium crawler to fetch real-time financial data from public databases and integrate it with internal Salesforce records.
  • Data Strategy: Solved a critical "Entity Resolution" problem (brand name vs. legal name) by re-engineering the matching logic to use Business Registration Numbers as the unique key.

Business Impact

Easier Information Retrieval
Faster Account Assignment

CorpFinder: Internal AI Product

User Pain Point (Product)

Data verification was inaccurate because each sales rep followed different naming conventions for Accounts (e.g., brand name vs. legal name), making standard search ineffective.

Product Solution

  • Product Vision: "A search tool that understands context, not just keywords."
  • Implementation: Integrated Gemini API (LLM) to handle semantic queries, enabling fuzzy matching and handling bilingual inputs (Korean/English).
  • UX Improvement: Deployed as a chat interface to lower the barrier to entry, bypassing complex Salesforce logins.

Business Impact

Outcome: Awarded Peer Bonus (Innovation). Recognized for improving Partner Experience (PX) and reducing support ticket volume.

Sales Signal Alert System

Problem Statement

Sales teams were missing "Golden Time" opportunities because identifying relevant investment news for 1,500+ clients manually was impossible. This information gap led to lost revenue potential.

Solution

  • Product Design: Engineered a workflow that extracts investment data tables directly from source HTML.
  • Filtering Logic: Implemented filtering logic to match internal client lists and identify relevant investment events.
  • Automated Delivery: Automated real-time alerts via Zapier and Workspace Flows to deliver actionable leads to sales reps instantly.

Business Impact

Outcome: Directly contributed to the sales pipeline by delivering 36+ high-value leads (investment events), enabling proactive sales engagement.

Product Growth & Monetization

Problem Statement (Growth)

Despite user acquisition, revenue growth was stagnant. Analysis revealed high churn in the onboarding flow for specific browsers (Edge: 46% conversion) and a mismatch in pricing for the core 40-50s demographic.

Strategic Action

  • Deep Data Analysis: Diagnosed the root cause of churn by correlating browser usage with drop-offs (identifying the Edge redirect issue). Uncovered the 'Willingness to Pay' of the 40-50s demographic through cohort behavior analysis.
  • Pricing Strategy: Restructured pricing tiers to align with value perception of 'Power Users', validated through A/B testing.
  • UX Optimization: Implemented browser-specific redirects and redesigned the tutorial UI with larger visuals to reduce friction for the target demographic.

Business Impact

Outcome: Achieved a 2x increase in Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) by fixing the "leaky bucket" in onboarding and optimizing the monetization model.
